All In One USB Drive was created to centralize critical system utilities and OS installers into a single, portable platform. The project included a curated collection of open-source and proprietary tools, multiboot setup instructions, and a step-by-step guide, providing convenience, reliability, and portability to technicians and IT administrators. Before this project, assembling a versatile USB toolkit required manual collection and setup of disparate tools, which was time-consuming and prone to errors. My goal was to deliver a unified, easy-to-maintain system, ensuring efficiency, accessibility, and comprehensive coverage. The final result is a fully functional multiboot USB drive, combining automated setup instructions, organized software libraries, and cross-platform compatibility, enabling users to address diagnostics, recovery, and installation needs seamlessly.

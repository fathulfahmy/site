Debt Collector was created to simplify personal debt management with a mobile-first approach. The project included features for adding debts, marking them resolved, deleting entries, and viewing both outstanding and historical records, providing clear visibility and control over financial obligations. Before this project, tracking personal debts required manual record-keeping or multiple disconnected tools, increasing errors and missed payments. My goal was to deliver a straightforward, accessible app, ensuring efficiency, accuracy, and usability were prioritized. The final result is an Android application that consolidates debt tracking into an intuitive interface, combining real-time updates and historical records to help users manage finances effectively.

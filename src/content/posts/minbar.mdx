Minbar was created to optimize the usability and visual clarity of Zebar’s top bar for GlazeWM users. The project included a compact layout, vibrant color adjustments, and accessible indicators for tiling and resizing, providing enhanced workflow efficiency and immediate visual feedback. Before this project, the default Zebar widgets were visually flat and lacked intuitive cues, limiting productivity. My goal was to refine the widget pack through targeted design and functional improvements, ensuring clarity, responsiveness, and accessibility were central to the solution. The final result is a streamlined, visually distinct top bar, combining color-coded interaction cues and a compact interface to deliver an effective experience for the GlazeWM community, now available on the Zebar Marketplace.

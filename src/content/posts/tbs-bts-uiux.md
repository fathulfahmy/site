The TBS-BTS UI/UX Redesign was created to enhance usability and visual consistency across the transit application. The project included redesigned login pages, search and ticket listings, interactive directory navigation, and a modernized news feed, providing an intuitive and efficient experience for commuters. Before this project, the interface was inconsistent and visually outdated, reducing user engagement and clarity. My goal was to apply design best practices with reusable components, ensuring accessibility, clarity, and visual coherence were central. The final result is a polished, user-friendly interface, combining card-style layouts, vibrant status indicators, and interactive navigation to improve commuter experience.
